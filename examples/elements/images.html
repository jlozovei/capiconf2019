Good case

Images may be tricky for accessibility, but there's no secret: using alt attribute and a correct markup will solve the problem.

Your images must have the alt attribute declareted, even though it would be empty. This is an accessibility/SEO trick. The "empty alt" images are ignored by screen readers.

Bad case

The image below doesn't has an alt attribute declared. So, the screen reader would read it as "image"
